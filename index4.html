Nibha Akireddy is a student at Stanford University studying Electrical Engineering and Art. When she’s not creating games at Girls Who Code, she likes to paint in her garage and gain experience as a burgeoning French fry connoisseur. She hopes to one day join the scores of entrepreneurs in the tech industry and find her own way to break the glass ceiling.
Phoebe Yin is a student at UC Berkeley studying Computer Science and Business. When her friends proclaim she is acting "so phoebe," it's usually a 50/50 coin-toss between being a compliment or a roast. She sees herself as a coder, orator, and Oscar-nominated-movie-enthusiast. Her most ambitious goal is to one day enter Time’s 100 Most Influential list.
Sadie Byun is a student at USC studying Business and Social Entrepreneurship. She has a great love for corgis that surpasses any love you’ve ever seen. As well as being passionate about adorable fuzzy creatures, Sadie enjoys being around others and coding to make an impact.
Zoe Ellyse Del Rosario is student at USC studying Design and Web Technologies. She’s an active doodler and is passionate about music. She tolerates math and loves to write about unrealistic, elaborate situations. You can find her at either the Barnes and Nobles in Emeryville or at Milk Tea Lab in Pleasant Hill.
